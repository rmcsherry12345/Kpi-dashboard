How to read this dashboard

Trend Direction
Green = Rising
Red = Falling
Gray = Flat

Direction compares the 6-week average vs the 10-week average. A difference within 0.5 is considered flat.

Revenue vs Target Categories

Many revenue metrics (Load vs budget, Co Tractor LH Rev, Trailer LH Rev) are measured versus target.

Negative average = shortfall vs target.
Rising (green) trend on a negative average = the gap is closing.

Periods: 10-week = weeks ending 4/11–6/13/2026   •   6-week = weeks ending 5/9–6/13/2026 (most recent 6 weeks).   "—" = no recorded activity for that terminal/category.
